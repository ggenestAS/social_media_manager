# Pilier 3 — Série de calculs (Carrousel)

**Format:** 1080 × 1350 px portrait (render at 540 × 675), ×6 slides  
**File name tag:** `carrousel`

---

## Concept

A 6-slide quiz carousel: swipe through calculations, answer mentally, swipe to verify. Thematically coherent series (same family: all ×25, all percentages, etc.).

---

## Series selection advice

Good families: ×25, ×11, ×5, simple percentages (10%/20%/15%/5%), perfect squares.
Avoid mixing families within a series.

## Caption formula

"Série #[N] — [X] secondes par calcul ⏱\n\nCommente ton score 👇 (🔥 = 4/4 | 💪 = 3/4)\n\n#calculmental #albertprep"
