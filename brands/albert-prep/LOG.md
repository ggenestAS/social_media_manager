## 2026-07-02 · READOUT — June 25 – Jul 1 analytics (first real evaluation)

Basis for everything above. Key findings (IG per-post, Postiz analytics):

- **Format is the lever:** quiz-rafale reels on broad themes averaged ~1,440
  views; single-calc "Tu as X secondes" reels averaged 165 (~9×). 13
  single-calc posts ≈ 1 mediocre quiz in total views.

  entangled with format and posting time in June data.
