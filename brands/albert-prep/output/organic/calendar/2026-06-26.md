## Measurement note

QCM is the cheapest engagement instrument we have:
- **Feed/Reels:** read the A/B/C split from comments — which distractor wins tells
  us which misconception is real.
- **Story (#003):** use the native IG **Quiz sticker** over the options → instant
  answer distribution, no manual tally.

Feed the winning misconceptions back into `astuce` posts (e.g. if `140 €` beats
`144 €` on #002, the compounding trap deserves its own astuce).
