# EXP-2026-07-05-echelle

**Hypothesis:** A difficulty ladder ("Du CE2 à la Prépa" — 5 questions, each
one school level harder) will out-reach the flat quiz because the framing is
identity bait: everyone from collégiens to parents can play, and the natural
comment is *the level you died at*, which is more social than a score.

**Deviation axis:** content angle + hook — progression narrative instead of
flat drill; level tags (CE2 → 5e → 3e → Terminale → Prépa) on each question;
outro asks « Jusqu'où tu as tenu ? » instead of « Combien sur 5 ? ».

**Breaks from:** `x-quiz-rafale` — same engine but adds per-question `tag`
rendering (gold level label) and outro override; audience widens beyond the
lycée core on purpose.

**What "worked" would look like:** reach beyond follower base (broad appeal),
comment quality = level names («tombé à la Terminale 😭»), shares to
classmates/parents. Risk to watch: reach without lycéen relevance — check
whether profile visits/follows still come.

**Platform(s):** ig, fb, tiktok — daily 15:00 Paris slot (19:00 on Jul 5).
