# EXP-2026-07-05-piege-correction

**Hypothesis:** A single-question "piège" reel that shows the tempting wrong
answer struck through (the brand's correction gesture, full-screen) will beat
the quiz-rafale baseline on reach per post, because being wrong is more
shareable/commentable than being fast — the viewer either falls in the trap
(comments) or sends it to someone who will.

**Deviation axis:** structure + hook — one question instead of five; the
*wrong* answer is the content; reveal happens on-screen (quiz-rafale never
shows answers). 10 s total (5 s chrono → correction reveal), vs 28 s.

**Breaks from:** `reel` (answer reveal but no wrong-answer framing) and
`x-quiz-rafale` (multi-question, no reveal). First format to put the
correction gesture (strike-through → red italic → green ✓) full-screen in a
reel.

**What "worked" would look like:** views ≥ quiz-rafale same-day baseline
(~1,300), higher comment rate ("je suis tombé dedans"), watch for shares.
Short 10 s loop should also raise completion/replays.

**Platform(s):** ig, fb, tiktok — daily 09:00 Paris slot (12:00 on Jul 5).
