# Prep Albert (prep-ai) — brand definition

**Product:** Prep, "the exam agent" — an AI agent that builds a revision plan
back-from-exam-day and trains students daily until they're ready. Any exam;
launch focus: the French bac. Full product context:
[`context/product.md`](context/product.md).
Landing: <https://prepai.albertschool.com>.

## Mission

Turn exam revision from a panic into a plan: the student tells Prep their
exam, Prep sequences every week until the day J and trains them in short
daily sessions. On exam day you're the only model in the room — Prep's job
is getting the syllabus into your head, not doing your homework.

## Audience

- **Lycéens (Première / Terminale)** — bac spé, français 1re, Grand oral;
  anxiety: "I don't know where to start, everything feels urgent."
- **Parents of lycéens** — can't revise in their child's place; want method,
  visible progress, and an alternative to €40/h tutoring.
- (Secondary: university entrance & standard tests — SAT, TOLC, concours.)

## Visual identity — "The agent"

Strict monochrome, Klar-style editorial. Dark is the brand default: near-black
paper, near-white ink, one serif moment (the greeting), everything else Inter.
The signature is **the chat itself**: Prep's round graduate mascot, agent
message bubbles, quick-reply chips, and the raised composer bar. The landing
IS the agent — ads should look like talking to it.
Palette, fonts, CSS, mascot SVG and artboard conventions are the contract in
[`templates/design-system.md`](templates/design-system.md). Use it verbatim.

## Voice & tone

- **Language:** French for the FR-highschool market (all launch content).
  English exists on the product but not in this brand's social output yet.
- **Tone:** calm, direct, a little wry — an agent that plans, not a coach that
  cheers. Short sentences. No emoji storms, no urgency theatrics.
- **Honesty is the positioning:** never promise grades or claim the AI writes
  exams. The line is "ChatGPT does your homework for you. Prep gets you ready
  when it counts." Early access is free, no credit card — say so plainly.
- **Every claim verified.** No invented statistics, no fabricated
  testimonials, no fake countdowns. Product-truth only: plan to day J, daily
  short sessions, real-format mock exams, free early access.
- **CTA:** « Construire mon plan — gratuit » (or angle variants). Destination
  is always a campaign LP (see context/product.md), never the bare homepage —
  attribution depends on it.
